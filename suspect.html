The Suspect

← Back to Films

Synopsis:
I made this short film in my senior year as a two-part episodic due to time constraints. As part of my school’s Friday Specials segment, we had to produce a 5–7 minute story that addressed a moral dilemma. I decided to put my spin on it by creating a detective noir about a cyberbully — inspired by my school’s notorious history of online harassment. I was the director, writer, cinematographer, DP, and editor.
