The Suspect

← Back to Films

Synopsis:
I made this short film in my senior year as a two part episodic due to time constraints as I was part of my school's school news and there were segments called Friday specials where we had to make a 5-7 minute special which had to detail a moral dilemma. I decided to put my spin on by making a detective noir investigating a cyberbully which was inspired by my school's notorious history of bullying through pages and certain accounts. I was the director, writer, cinematographer, DP, and editor.
